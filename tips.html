Tips 'n Tricks

Some tips and tricks to make life a bit easier for you...

Try to avoid vertical pan/swipe

Vertical panning is used to scroll your page, and some browsers don't send events so Hammer isn't able to recognize these gestures.

Test on a real device

Sometimes Hammer just needs some fine-tuning, like the swipe velocity or some other thresholds. Also, for better performance your should try to keep you callbacks as simple as possible.
